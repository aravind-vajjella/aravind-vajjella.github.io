1.Online Library Management System

The Online Library Management System is a web-based application that allows students to search, borrow, and return books. It helps librarians manage the collection of books digitally.

Tech Stack:Html,css,javaScript,Java(servlets,JDBC),MySQL

2.Hospital Appointment System

The Hospital Appointment System is a web-based full-stack application designed to streamline the process of booking and managing medical appointments. The system provides different interfaces for patients, doctors, and administrators, ensuring smooth interaction and better hospital management.

Patients can register, log in, and book appointments with doctors based on availability. They can also check the status of their requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected).

Doctors can log in to view appointment requests, approve or reject them, and manage their schedules.

Admin can manage doctor and patient records, oversee the entire appointment system, and ensure smooth hospital operations.

Tech Stack:Html,css,javaScript,Java(servlets,JDBC),MySQL
